Pokémon Types

  • Normal

    Description: Normal types are versatile with a broad range of moves. They're reliable but not especially effective against other types.

    Weak to: Fighting

  • Fire

    Description: Fire types are aggressive, specializing in strong attacks that deal damage by setting things on fire! Famous examples include Charizard and Arcanine.

    Weak to: Water, Ground, Rock

  • Water

    Description: Water types are adaptable and excel at handling fire with their fluid and cool moves. Examples include Squirtle and Gyarados.

    Weak to: Electric, Grass

  • Electric

    Description: Electric types are fast and powerful. Pikachu is the perfect example, using quick and shocking moves to stun opponents!

    Weak to: Ground

  • Grass

    Description: Grass types are closely tied to nature, using growth-related abilities. They're typically weak against Fire but strong defensively. Bulbasaur and Venusaur lead the way.

    Weak to: Fire, Ice, Poison, Flying, Bug

  • Ice

    Description: Ice types specialize in freezing opponents. They excel at freezing and slowing down foes, like Lapras and Glaceon.

    Weak to: Fire, Fighting, Rock, Steel

  • Fighting

    Description: Fighting types are known for their physical strength, focusing on martial arts. They use punches and kicks, like Machop and Lucario.

    Weak to: Flying, Psychic, Fairy

  • Poison

    Description: Poison types specialize in inflicting status effects. They use toxins and poisons to debilitate opponents, like Muk and Venusaur.

    Weak to: Ground, Psychic

  • Ground

    Description: Ground types are strong and solid. They rely on powerful physical moves and are resistant to Electric attacks, with Pokémon like Golem and Swampert.

    Weak to: Water, Ice, Grass

  • Flying

    Description: Flying types are quick and nimble. They can easily avoid Ground-type moves, and Pokémon like Pidgeot and Fearow represent this type.

    Weak to: Electric, Ice, Rock

  • Psychic

    Description: Psychic types use mind-bending abilities to manipulate their enemies. Mewtwo and Alakazam are iconic Psychic types.

    Weak to: Bug, Ghost, Dark

  • Bug

    Description: Bug types rely on quick and overwhelming attacks. They swarm and often cause status effects, as seen with Scyther and Beedrill.

    Weak to: Fire, Flying, Rock

  • Rock

    Description: Rock types are defensive and can take a lot of damage. Pokémon like Onix and Tyranitar are known for their tough nature.

    Weak to: Water, Grass, Fighting, Ground, Steel

  • Ghost

    Description: Ghost types are tricky and mysterious. They use illusions and avoid Normal-type attacks, like Gengar and Mimikyu.

    Weak to: Ghost, Dark

  • Dragon

    Description: Dragons are powerful and majestic. They have high stats and strong moves, with Pokémon like Dragonite and Garchomp being prime examples.

    Weak to: Ice, Dragon, Fairy

  • Dark

    Description: Dark types are sneaky and devious, specializing in trickery and surprise. Pokémon like Umbreon and Zoroark excel in this type.

    Weak to: Fighting, Bug, Fairy

  • Steel

    Description: Steel types are durable and resilient, known for their resistance to many types of attacks. Steelix and Aegislash are key representatives.

    Weak to: Fire, Fighting, Ground

  • Fairy

    Description: Fairy types are magical and charming, specializing in status effects. Pokémon like Togekiss and Sylveon represent this mystical type.

    Weak to: Steel, Poison
